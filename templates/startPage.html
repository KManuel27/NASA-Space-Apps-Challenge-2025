CRASH

Celestial Risk Assessment and Safety Hub

About

CRASH (Celestial Risk Assessment and Safety Hub) is a web application developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025. It is designed for scientists, researchers, and policymakers to model asteroid trajectories and visualise potential impact scenarios.

Leveraging real data from NASA and the CSGS, combined with robust physics-based simulations, CRASH delivers accurate, data-driven insights into the potential impacts of asteroid collisions. Users can analyse hazardous near-Earth objects (NEOs), explore their projected paths, and assess possible damage zones in an intuitive, interactive environment.

By merging scientific accuracy with accessible visualisation, CRASH bridges the gap between complex astrophysical data and actionable decision-making. This empowers scientists to develop innovative solutions, enables policymakers to design effective planetary defense strategies, and fosters greater public understanding of asteroid impact risks.
